Senior Technical Lead for Governmental Agency

Aladdin supported a government agency by sourcing a Senior Technical Lead for its major ICT transformation initiative that had urgent delivery deadlines. Prior to engaging Aladdin, the role had been advertised nationally for a substantial period. Aladdin screened multiple candidates and provided a suitably qualified, security cleared candidate within three weeks. Aladdin managed onboarding, payroll and ongoing performance monitoring to support the placed candidate and the agency.

The contractor recently had their contract extended for a further 12 months due to quality performance and project impact.
