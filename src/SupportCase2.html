IT Support and Operations for a HR Compliance System

Aladdin was engaged to stabilise and enhance a governmental agency’s HR compliance system. Aladdin’s system developer analysed system data, identified incorrect process flows and recurring incidents, clarified missing requirements and implemented a structured action plan. The developer introduced a new IT-led incident management process which included triage and change management workflows, they fixed incidents root causes and provided relevant documentation to manage the solution internally, long-term.

As a result of the actions Aladdin’s system developer took, our client agency is seeing zero recurrences of incidents and a decrease in both responses and incident resolution timeframes. Data synchronisation with SAP has also improved, which has led to increased confidence in the accuracy and reliability of compliance reporting.
