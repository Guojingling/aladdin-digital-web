Support and operations services

Aladdin Digital provides ICT support and operations services to ensure our clients have access to stable, secure and efficient business systems. We deliver end-to-end incident management, triage process, troubleshooting procedures and prompt system outage remediation while preventing recurrence through root-cause analysis and workflow improvements. Our team leverages their extensive industrial experience to assess application health status, improve system reliability and proactively strengthen security. We also help our clients adopt cost-effective and scalable technologies to fulfil their evolving business needs. Our team is experienced across multiple platforms and we specialise in system support establishment, BAU operational support and cloud-based monitoring, troubleshooting and security management.

Aladdin was urgently engaged after a local institute lost critical email data during a services upgrade. Aladdin deployed an onsite technician to investigate the cause of the data loss, identify recoverable data and implement a secure cloud-based solution to future-proof its email system.

Aladdin restored normal operations with four hours of engagement and recovered 80% of critical lost data before implementing an Azure-based solution with cloud backup, automated archiving and multi-region disaster recovery.

The institute has now eliminated manual backups of email and significantly reduced the risk of similar incidents. Aladdin provides quarterly checks of CI’s email environment to ensure ongoing stability.

Aladdin was engaged to stabilise and enhance a governmental agency’s Lighthouse HR compliance system. Aladdin’s system developer analysed system data, identified incorrect process flows and recurring incidents, clarified missing requirements and implemented a structured action plan. The developer introduced a new IT-led incident management process which included triage and change management workflows, they fixed incidents root causes and provided all relevant documentation support NEMA’s ability to manage the solution internally, long-term.

As a result of the actions Aladdin’s system developer took, our client agency is seeing zero recurrences of incidents and a decrease in both responses and incident resolution timeframes. Data synchronisation with SAP has also improved, which has led to increased confidence in the accuracy and reliability of compliance reporting.
