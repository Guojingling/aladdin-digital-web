Talent hire, recruitment and related services

Aladdin Digital offers comprehensive labour hire and recruitment services to provide highly skilled IT professionals to government clients. Our services include role scoping, candidate sourcing, technical screening, security and work-rights verification, interview preparation, onboarding and ongoing performance monitoring.

We can deliver both seller-sourced and buyer-sourced labour hire models and can also supply seller-sourced workers under buyer-managed arrangements. Our recruitment approach focuses on technical merit, compliance with government standards and long-term capability uplift and we source personnel with strong expertise in solution design, technical implementation and BAU support.
