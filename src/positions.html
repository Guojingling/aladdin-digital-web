Solution Architect

The project requires the services of 2 x Solutions Architects in the change program. The Solution Architect Data Information Transformation (DIT) will work across delegated Stream(s), work with the DIT Program to deliver fit for purpose Solution Architectures. This would include but not limited to working within a multidisciplinary team of Business Analysts, Program Architects, Project and Program Managers and Business Stakeholders.

The role will:

  • Develop solution architectures in accordance with Architecture Principles, reference architectures and Security guidelines.
  • Engage with internal partners(s) in developing solution architectures.
  • Work with the broader program team to ensure the overall architecture is consistent with, and fully supports, the overall business and platform architectures for the program.
  • Assist in specifying the infrastructure requirements and architecture required to support the services architecture.
  • Assist in estimating budgetary resources, schedules, and costs required for the solutions.
  • Assist in developing patterns when requested.
  • Work with internal stakeholders and external stakeholders in producing Solution Architecture and assisting with Detail Design deliverables.
  • Work with the various roles and work streams within the program.
  • Comply with all client work, health and safety policies and procedures, and take reasonable care for your own health and safety and that of employees, contractors and visitors who may be affected by your conduct.

    • Essential Criteria:

  • Awareness of IT security concepts and standards.
  • 5+ years of Solution Architecture experience.
  • Ability to build relationships with senior managers and staff and an ability to communicate effectively with both business and technical staff.
  • Understanding of how to design services to meet security and useability requirements.
  • Due to security clearance requirements for this role, candidate must have NV1 Security Clearance.
